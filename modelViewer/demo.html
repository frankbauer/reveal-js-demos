3D Models using <ModelViewer>

A Reveal.JS Demo showcasing the ModelViewer-Plugin.

Suits

Here are just some 3D Models* of a space suit.
Presented using the <ModelViewer> Library.

*Assets from <ModelViewer> under the Apache Licence

We can use fragments to change the view of our models.
We can also change the perspective for just one of the models
There is even a Truntable Animation
Sometimes we want to change what we are looking at
We can also annotate our models (using the Editor) ...
... reveal annotations as a fragment ...
... and focus on specific annotations.
When we are done, we can hide the annotations

and

allow users to move the camera

Or we can tell a story using the 3D-Model...
